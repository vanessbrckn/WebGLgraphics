CSE 470 HW #2: Pick a Cube

Author: Vanessa Bracken date: 03/02/2021
Description: This program takes a prototype cube, centers it on the canvas, and then duplicates
the cube around the center cube using a for loop and multiple redraws. The user may click an outer
cube to replace the middle one, speed up the rotations, and make the cubes larger
